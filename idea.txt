Assignment idea

Class: Survey, Candidate, Question
- Candidate class:
-- first Name
-- Last Name
-- Phone Number
-- Email address

- Question class:
-- Question
-- Answers:
--- Agree
--- Slightly Agree
--- Slightly Disagree
--- Disagree
--- prefer not to answer

- Survey Class:
-- Survey Name
-- Survey Description
-- Questions


Helper Class:
- find the most given answer on a survey
- print a survey's result, the number of each answer/question
- find the answers given by a candidate on a survey
- add/remove questions to an existing survey
- checks if the question is answered by less than 50% of the candidates, and if not it will be removed
- validates survey
-- must have at least 10 questions
-- can have at most 40 questions
-- each question has to be unique
